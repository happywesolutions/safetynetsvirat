About Virat

Build Your Safe and Secure Home without Compromising the View.

At Virat Safety Nets & Invisible Grills, we craft safety solutions using premium materials — 316 marine-grade stainless steel cables, UV-stabilised HDPE nets, and aluminium hardware engineered to last a decade outdoors.

Every installation is precision-fitted by trained technicians and inspected for tension, alignment and finish. The result: invisible-from-outside protection that keeps your family safe while preserving the view, breeze and natural light you love.

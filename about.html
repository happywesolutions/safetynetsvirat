Our Story

Safety Without Compromise

Virat Safety Nets & Invisible Grills was founded with a single mission — to make Indian homes safer without compromising the view, breeze or natural light that make a home feel alive.

Over 12+ years we’ve completed 1812+ installations across 150+ locations, becoming the trusted choice for families, builders and architects who refuse to settle for less.

Every project — whether a single window net or an entire high-rise — is treated with the same precision: premium materials, trained installers, and a finish that makes the safety practically invisible.
